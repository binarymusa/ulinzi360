Ulinzi 360 Consultancy and Security Management Limited is a Kenya-based advisory firm specializing in risk governance, intelligence analysis, and institutional security strategy.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2024

Ulinzi 360 was established in 2024 to address a growing gap in advisory-led security and structured risk management across institutions and organizations in Kenya.

The firm was founded on the principle that modern security challenges require intelligence, governance frameworks, and strategic foresight — not just operational response or equipment-based solutions.

From its inception, Ulinzi 360 has operated as a vendor-neutral consultancy, focusing exclusively on analysis, advisory, and institutional readiness.
